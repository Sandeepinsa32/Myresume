How to Speed Up Your Computer ?
 
 

Welcome to this new blog post. Almost everyone has a computer or laptop at the time of this technology. There are a lot of problems while working in it, in which the most problem is the speed of the system. When we get a new laptop or computer, its speed is very fast but after a few months the speed slows down. Does your computer work slow? Are you worried about its slow speed, if yes then this post is for you.” As we all know that in this digital age, technical things like computers, smartphones etc. have become an important part of our lives. In such a situation, it is sure to come with some problems. For this, it is important that you take full care of your computer system. And always keep updating your system. I will tell you how you can clean the memory of your computer or laptop and make it super fast. Apart from this, we will also tell how we can close an app’s running in the background that we do not use . So let’s know how to make Computer Faster?

 
  1. Clean the unused memory from the computer.
  2. Stop useless programs from starting at startup.
  3. Delete Temporary Files from Computer.
  4. Delete your browsers’ caches and cookies.
  5. Install Antivirus in your computer.
